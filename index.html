Patient History Dashboard

This is the Patient History Dashboard. It provides us valuable information about our Patients background, such as their age, income, and gender.

Based on these insights, I would recommend stakeholders to consider targeted interventions for patients in lower-income brackets, particularly among older age groups. This could involve developing programs to provide financial support, access to resources, or specialized healthcare services tailored to the specific needs of these segments.

Patient Provider Dashboard

This is the Patient Provider Dashboard. It provides us valuable information about Medicare payments and Coverage Costs.

Based on insights, stakeholders should explore DRG definitions and associated Medicare payments. Identify high-paying DRGs, analyze reasons, and optimize costs within medical categories. Use cost and payment insights to find cost-saving opportunities. Streamline processes, optimize resources, and negotiate favorable reimbursement rates without compromising care quality.
